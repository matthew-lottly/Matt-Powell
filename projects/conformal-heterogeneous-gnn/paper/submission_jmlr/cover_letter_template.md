Dear Editor,

Please consider the enclosed manuscript entitled "STRATA: Conformal Prediction with Heterogeneous Message-Passing Calibration for Infrastructure Risk Assessment" by Matthew Powell for publication in JMLR.

This manuscript introduces STRATA, a framework that combines heterogeneous graph neural networks with a novel Conformal Heterogeneous Message-Passing (CHMP) calibration scheme to produce topology-adaptive, distribution-free prediction intervals for node-level infrastructure risk assessment. We demonstrate empirical results on synthetic multi-utility graphs and the ACTIVSg200 power-grid test case.
